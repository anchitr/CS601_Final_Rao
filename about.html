THE DEVELOPER BEHIND THE COMPUTER

My Background

Hi! I'm Anchit Rao, a 2nd year graduate student studying Computer Information Systems at Boston University. I'm passionate about building software that makes a difference in people's lives. My story starts in the metropolitan city of Bangalore, India. I was born and raised in Bangalore for five years before my family moved to the United States. We moved to the sunny state of California, where I still live today. I attended elementary, middle, and high school in California. I graduated from high school in 2013, attended my local community college for three years before transferring to Indiana University in Bloomington to study Accounting. While I was there, I discovered the power of Python programming and took a class, which sparked my interest in programming. I ultimately ended up switching my major to Informatics, and completed my undergraduate studies in 2019.

My Current Work

Since then, I've been working as a Software Engineer for HCL Technologies, consulting at Western Digital. I was brought on to work on a project that was meant to replace a legacy system for tracking and managing every serial number of every hard drive that Western Digital manufactures. I was responsible for designing the front-end of the application using Figma. I also worked on modeling and creating the backend database in Oracle SQL Developer. Recently, I've been utilizing Apache Airflow and Python to automate multiple data pipelines for our e-commerce site's search backend.
